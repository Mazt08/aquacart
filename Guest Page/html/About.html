Our Journey

How It All Began

Founded in 2018 to bring pure, responsibly bottled water to local communities. We combine modern purification with traditional spring sourcing to keep taste and minerals intact.

Quality First

Every drop undergoes rigorous multi-stage filtration and UV sterilization, exceeding international safety standards.

Local Roots

Sourced from protected natural springs in our region, supporting local ecosystems and communities.

Always Fresh

Bottled daily and delivered within 48 hours, ensuring maximum freshness with every sip.

Our Milestones

2018

Company Founded

Aqua Cart was established with a vision to provide locally sourced, purified water that maintains natural mineral content while meeting the highest safety standards.

2019

First Production Plant

Opened our state-of-the-art purification facility with multi-stage filtration, UV sterilization, and automated quality control systems.

2020

ISO 22000:2018 Certification

Achieved international certification for food safety management, validating our commitment to quality and safe production practices.

2024

50,000 Bottles Milestone

Reached 50,000+ bottles delivered while launching eco-friendly packaging and carbon-neutral delivery program.

Sustainability

Our Commitment to the Planet

We believe in responsible business practices that protect our environment for future generations.

100% Recyclable

All bottles are made from 100% recyclable PET plastic. We partner with local recycling programs for proper disposal.

Carbon Neutral

Our delivery fleet runs on electric and hybrid vehicles, offsetting 100% of emissions through verified carbon credits.

Water Conservation

We recycle 95% of water used in production and implement rainwater harvesting at all facilities.

Spring Protection

We work with environmental agencies to protect natural spring ecosystems and ensure long-term sustainability.
